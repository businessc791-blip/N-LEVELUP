Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Promise

Kid Racers is made for fun! We don't ask for your name or create accounts. Your privacy is super important to us.

What Info We Use

  • Game Scores: Your high scores are saved on your own computer or tablet. We can't see them!
  • Contact Form: If you or your parents send us a message, we'll use your email only to write back.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. You can play all our games without them.

Your Choices

  • You can ask a grown-up to help you clear your browser's data to erase your high scores and start fresh.
  • Using a private or incognito window in your browser means scores won't be saved.

Questions?

If you have any questions, please ask a parent to help you use the contact form on our home page.
