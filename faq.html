Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Kid Racers.

What do I need to play?

Just a web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) on a computer, tablet, or phone. You don't need to download anything!

How are my scores saved?

Your best scores are saved right on your device in the browser. They are private to you and not shared with anyone.

Can I reset my scores?

Yes! If you want to start over, you can clear your browser's site data. Ask a grown-up for help if you're not sure how.

What controls are supported?

Use the Arrow Keys (Up/Down) on a keyboard to move your car. On a phone or tablet, you can just tap the screen!

Will it work on my device?

Our games are made to work well on lots of different devices, from new computers to older phones. It should be smooth and fun for everyone!
